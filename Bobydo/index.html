About

Bobydo Inc. is a Canada-based software consulting firm specializing in enterprise application development, pipeline SCADA systems, and AI-powered analytics. Principal consultant Shenyi Bao brings over 20 years of hands-on experience designing, developing, and supporting mission-critical systems across energy, government, and insurance sectors.

We build enterprise-grade full-stack applications using C#/.NET, Python, React, and Angular — from clean RESTful APIs and distributed services to modern cloud-native deployments on Azure. Our work spans the full spectrum: industrial SCADA systems and real-time telemetry, scalable data pipelines and Power BI analytics, and AI-powered RAG solutions that turn your operational data into actionable intelligence.

Strong background in DevOps, CI/CD automation, and testing frameworks — including regression and integration testing for enterprise SCADA deployments. Collaborative Agile team member focused on automation, operational visibility, and enterprise-grade solutions that ensure reliability, scalability, and maintainability across industrial and cloud systems.
